How it works

Designer Consultation

Make an appointment with us, and after meeting you, we will have a complete plan from our designers for you within 48 hours. There is always a Designer on Duty at Desire.

Production

We offer private guidelines and assistance for measuring your space accurately. Proper measurements allow your new furniture to get through your doors and fit right where you want it.

Measurements

We are using an ERP system to oversee the entire process from the purchase of raw materials, to the delivery of the final product. Time production depends on the avialibility of the materials and size of the demand.
