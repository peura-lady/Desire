About Us

Desire is the premier brand for high-end indoor and outdoor furniture for every aspect of your home

Desire is well known for its unmistakably unique and authentic look and feel. Every item in the collection is made with the finest materials, such as genuine buffalo leather from Italy, solid rustical and reclaimed oak (which has been ageing for hundreds of years), and carefully selected fabrics.

At Desire we believe that our collections should inspire you and be a complete experience. It is all about helping you express your individual style, so talk to one of our associates about creating your own custom furniture.
